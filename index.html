Why We Made KeyGuard

We created KeyGuard as when we were looking for an auth system for our product the only ones that we could find were auths that made you pay for the most basic features and so because of this we made a free version which did satisfy our needs. So you may be asking, Why did you make it open source? This is so that other people can help add more things that we didnt even think about.

FAQ

Can I Just Add Your Bot To My Server?

Yeah you can but we recommend you self-host the bot as otherwise people can see your keys.

Which Language Is The Bot / SRC Written In?

KeyGuard is mostly written in Python with a little bit of HTML.

Which Ports Do I Need To Open?

The key database runs on port 8080. However you shouldn't need to do anything for these ports to work.

If I Need Support Can I Get In Touch?

Of course you can but just remember that our staff cannot be online to support 24/7. If you want to take your chances with the wait time then you can ask any question in our discord server or create an issue on our github.

Is KeyGuard Uncrackable?

KeyGuard is by no means uncrackable, as with every auth it relies on the protection of the loader itself.
